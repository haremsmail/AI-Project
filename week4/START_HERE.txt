💡 TIPS FOR SUCCESS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Basic Usage:
  • Start with default parameters
  • Use speed slider to slow down for observation
  • Try moving goal to different positions
  • Increase particles gradually (5 → 10 → 20)

Understanding PSO:
  • Low w makes particles settle quickly
  • High c1 makes particles remember personal history
  • High c2 makes particles follow the swarm
  • Balance all three for best results

Visualization:
  • Arrows show particle velocity direction
  • Pink star is always the goal
  • Green crosshair is best found position
  • Numbered circles are particles
  • Faded lines show recent movement

Troubleshooting:
  • Always click START before animation begins
  • History shows iterations only after running
  • Reset clears everything (use if confused)
  • Pause to examine canvas at any moment
  • Check all input fields have valid numbers
